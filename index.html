The Framework

Four pillars hold up a life.

Body. Order. Responsibility. Mastery. Most people are weak in at least one and don't know it. Remoralization measures all four, every week, and refuses to let you ignore the one you've been avoiding.

I · Body
Condition & Capability
The honest mirror. The run that proves you're still strong. A soft body produces a soft mind.
Heracles
II · Order
Environment & Systems
Your space, your presentation, your systems. The world reflects you correctly because you have ordered it to.
Themis
III · Responsibility
Duties & Follow-Through
The duties you can't delegate. The people who depend on you showing up. Strength is being someone others can rely on.
Aeneas
IV · Mastery
Pursuit & Deep Work
The thing you're building that no one else can build for you. Mastery isn't talent — it's working when no one's watching.
Daedalus
How it works

A monthly cycle of commit, work, reflect.

  • Set a 3-year vision — not a goal, a clear picture of who you're becoming.
  • Lock in one monthly goal. Daedalus, the master craftsman, judges your sessions against it.
  • Weekly check-ins keep you honest across all four pillars.
  • At month-end, a review you cannot skip. Hit, partial, or missed — and Daedalus tells you what he saw.
  • An archetype rotation keeps the work from going stale. The Oracle sees across all four.

"Thirty days are spent. Tell me what you built."

— Daedalus

Overview

Remoralization is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

Data We Collect

  • Account information: email address used to create your account
  • Health data: body measurements, waist measurements, and run data you manually enter
  • Photos: condition check-in photos and house spot photos you take within the app
  • Usage data: your ratings, goals, reflections, and progress entries

How We Use Your Data

  • To provide the app's features and track your progress across the four pillars
  • To generate personalized feedback from the archetype system
  • Your data is never sold to third parties

Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Supabase. Photos are stored in Supabase Storage.

AI Features

The app uses Anthropic's Claude API to generate archetype responses. Your pillar data is sent to this API to generate responses. No personally identifiable information is included.

Your Rights

You can delete your account and all associated data by contacting us at dylankundert@gmail.com.
