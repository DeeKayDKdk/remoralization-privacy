Privacy Policy — Last updated April 27, 2026

Overview

Remoralization is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

Data We Collect

  • Account information: email address used to create your account
  • Health data: body measurements, waist measurements, and run data you manually enter
  • Photos: condition check-in photos and house spot photos you take within the app
  • Usage data: your ratings, goals, reflections, and progress entries

How We Use Your Data

  • To provide the app's features and track your progress across the four pillars
  • To generate personalized feedback from the archetype system
  • Your data is never sold to third parties

Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Supabase. Photos are stored in Supabase Storage.

AI Features

The app uses Anthropic's Claude API to generate archetype responses. Your pillar data is sent to this API to generate responses. No personally identifiable information is included.

Your Rights

You can delete your account and all associated data by contacting us at dylankundert@gmail.com
