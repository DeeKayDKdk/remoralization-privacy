The Framework

Four pillars hold up a life.

Body. Order. Responsibility. Mastery. Most people drift in at least one without realizing it. Remoralization measures all four, every week, and refuses to let you ignore the one you've been avoiding.

I · Body
Condition & Capability
The honest mirror. The run that proves you're still strong. A soft body produces a soft mind.
Heracles
II · Order
Environment & Systems
Your space, your presentation, your systems. The world reads you correctly because you've made it order.
Themis
III · Responsibility
Duties & Follow-Through
The duties you can't delegate. The people who depend on you showing up. Strength is being someone others can rely on.
Aeneas
IV · Mastery
Pursuit & Deep Work
The thing you're building that no one else can build for you. Mastery isn't talent — it's working when no one's watching.
Daedalus
How it works

A monthly cycle of commit, work, reflect.

  • Set a 3-year vision — not a goal, a clear picture of who you're becoming.
  • Face your body honestly. Heracles measures condition and capability; the mirror does not lie.
  • Order your environment. Themis judges what your space and systems say about you.
  • Carry your duties. Aeneas tracks the people and obligations that depend on you showing up.
  • Pursue your craft. Daedalus, the master craftsman, judges your deep work against a monthly goal.
  • Weekly check-ins keep all four pillars honest. The Oracle sees across them.
  • At month-end, a review you cannot skip. Hit, partial, or missed — and your guides tell you what they saw.

"Thirty days are spent. Tell me what you built."
